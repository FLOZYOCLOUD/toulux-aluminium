About Toulux™

Based in the heart of Al Aroui (Nador, Morocco), Toulux™ has established itself as a premier provider of high-quality aluminum solutions.

With a commitment to craftsmanship and modern architectural standards, we specialize in supplying and installing premium aluminum profiles, composite panels (ACP), and insulating systems that improve both the aesthetics and energy efficiency of buildings.

Our track record includes over 230+ completed projects and 90+ successful installations across the country. We don't just sell products; we provide professional, tailor-made installation services to ensure every door, window, and facade meets the highest expectations of our clients.

Our Mission

"To empower modern Moroccan architecture by providing reliable, durable, and innovative aluminum solutions, backed by superior customer service and nationwide accessibility."
